# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 10, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Colors** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares
information when you use our mobile application (the "Game") in compliance with Google Play's Data
Safety requirements.

## 1. Data Collection and Sharing

To provide a functional, stable, and ad-supported game experience, we use third-party SDKs (Google
AdMob and Firebase). These services collect and share data as described below. We do not collect
personal identifiers like your name, email address, or phone number.

### A. Location Data

* **Data Type**: Coarse Location (e.g., city or country).
* **Status**: Collected and Shared.
* **Purpose**: Analytics and showing relevant advertisements.
* **How it's collected**: Derived from your IP address by third-party SDKs.

### B. Device and Other Identifiers

* **Data Type**: Advertising ID (GAID), Firebase installation ID, and other device-specific
  identifiers.
* **Status**: Collected and Shared.
* **Purpose**: Advertising, Analytics, Fraud Prevention, Security, and App Functionality (e.g.,
  ensuring unique sessions).

### C. App Activity

* **Data Type**: App interactions (clicks, levels completed, session duration).
* **Status**: Collected and Shared.
* **Purpose**: Analytics and Advertising.

### D. Performance and Diagnostics

* **Data Type**: Crash logs (technical details about app crashes) and Diagnostics (app performance
  metrics).
* **Status**: Collected and Shared.
* **Purpose**: Analytics and App Functionality (diagnosing and fixing bugs).

## 2. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are integrated into the Game and handle the data mentioned above:

### Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob uses device identifiers and coarse location to provide
personalized ads and measure performance.

* [Google's Advertising Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

### Firebase (Analytics & Diagnostics)

We use Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Performance to monitor game stability
and improve user experience.

* [Google Analytics for Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## 3. Data Security and Retention

* **Encryption**: All data collected by third-party SDKs is encrypted in transit using
  industry-standard protocols (HTTPS).
* **Retention**: Data is retained as per the policies of Google and Firebase.
* **Sharing**: Data is shared with the service providers (Google/Firebase) for the purposes stated
  above (advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention).

## 4. European (GDPR) and California (CCPA/CPRA) Disclosures

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have specific rights
regarding your data.

### A. European Union (GDPR)

For users in the EEA, we rely on your consent to process data for personalized advertising and
analytics. We use the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to collect this consent. You have the
right to:

* **Withdraw Consent**: You can change your privacy preferences at any time within the app's
  settings or by clearing the app's cache.
* **Access and Portability**: You can request information about the data processed by our
  third-party partners.

### B. California (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not "sell" your personal information in the traditional sense. However, the use of advertising
identifiers may be considered a "sharing" of data under California law. You can opt-out of
personalized advertising through your device's "Opt out of Interest-Based Ads" or "Delete
Advertising ID" settings.

## 5. Your Choices

You can choose whether this data is collected or shared:

1. **Opt-Out of Personalized Ads**: Go to **Settings > Google > Ads** on your Android device to
   reset or delete your Advertising ID.
2. **Consent Form**: Users in regulated regions will be presented with a consent form upon launching
   the app.
3. **Clearing App Data**: This resets local identifiers used for analytics.
4. **Permissions**: You can manage app permissions in your device's settings menu.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with data, we
will take steps to delete it from our servers.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the
new Privacy Policy on this page.
